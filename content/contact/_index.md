## General Inquiries

**Email:** [editor@thepublicledgers.org](mailto:editor@thepublicledgers.org)

We aim to respond within 72 hours.

## Tips

If you have information about public financial records, government contracts, or institutional accountability issues that should be examined, we want to hear from you.

- **Email:** [editor@thepublicledgers.org](mailto:editor@thepublicledgers.org)
- **Signal:** Available on request for sensitive communications
- **Mail:** Contact us by email first for a mailing address

We protect the identity of confidential sources. See our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/) for details on how we handle communications.

---

## Corrections

If you believe we have published an error, please contact us with:

1. The specific claim you believe is incorrect
2. The page or investigation where it appears
3. Supporting documentation (primary source preferred)

**Email:** [editor@thepublicledgers.org](mailto:editor@thepublicledgers.org)
**Subject line:** "Correction request: [page title]"

We verify and publish corrections promptly. See our [corrections policy](/standards/#corrections-policy) and [corrections log](/corrections/).

---

## Right of Reply

If you are the subject of an investigation and wish to respond to findings before or after publication, contact us at:

**Email:** [editor@thepublicledgers.org](mailto:editor@thepublicledgers.org)
**Subject line:** "Right of reply: [investigation name]"

We seek comment before publication from all individuals and organizations named in findings that could be perceived as negative. Subjects are given a minimum of 72 hours to respond, with longer windows available on request. See our [right of reply procedures](/legal/#right-of-reply).

---

## Press Inquiries

Journalists seeking comment, background, or access to underlying datasets for their own reporting:

**Email:** [editor@thepublicledgers.org](mailto:editor@thepublicledgers.org)
**Subject line:** "Press inquiry: [topic]"

We believe accountability journalism works best when findings reach the widest possible audience. We are open to collaboration, data sharing, and co-publication with other newsrooms.

---

## Conflict of Interest Reports

If you believe The Public Ledger has an undisclosed conflict of interest related to any investigation, contact us at:

**Email:** [editor@thepublicledgers.org](mailto:editor@thepublicledgers.org)
**Subject line:** "Conflict of interest concern: [investigation name]"

See our [Ethics & Conflict of Interest Policy](/ethics/).
