---
title: "Privacy"
description: "How we handle visitor data."
---

The Public Ledgers does not sell, share, or monetize visitor data.

## Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how many people read our work and which investigations are most useful. No personally identifiable information is collected.

## Cookies

This site uses minimal or no cookies. If analytics are enabled, they may set a session cookie. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

## Tip security

If you contact us with a tip, we protect your identity. We do not share tip information with anyone outside the editorial process. For sensitive communications, we recommend using Signal.

## Data we publish

All data published on this site is derived from public records. We do not publish personal addresses, phone numbers, or other private contact information. When public filings contain such information, we aggregate or redact it.
